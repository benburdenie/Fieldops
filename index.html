What FieldOps Handles
🗂
Job Assignments
Assign crews and equipment to jobs. Dates, addresses, client info, notes — all in one place. Automatic status tracking from Not Started → In Progress → Complete.
🚫
Conflict Detection
Try to double-book a machine or truck — FieldOps blocks the save instantly and tells you exactly what's conflicting. No more showing up on-site without the equipment.
🔧
Equipment Status
Every piece of equipment shows its real-time status — Available, In Use, In Repair, or Crew Assigned. Know what's available before you schedule, not after.
📆
Crew Availability
Block off dates for vacation, training, or sick time. FieldOps will warn you if you try to schedule a crew during blocked dates — before it causes a problem.
☀️
Today's Overview
Open the app and immediately see every active job today, which crew is on it, what equipment is deployed, and what's sitting available.
🖨
Dispatch Sheet
One click generates a clean printable dispatch sheet for the day — crews, jobs, addresses, equipment, and any unavailability notes. Ready to hand to your foreman.
